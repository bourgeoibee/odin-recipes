Microwave Rice

Description

It's microwave rice you got this.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long-grain white rice

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Put the rice and 2 cups of water in a big enough bowl
  3. Microwave uncovered for 15-25 minutes
  4. If rice is not done, keep microwaving in 1-2 minute increments
  5. Let rest for another 5 minutes
